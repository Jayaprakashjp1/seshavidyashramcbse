About VPMS SELVI SHANKAR (SESHA) VIDYASHRAM SCHOOL

SELVI SHANKAR (SESHA) VIDYASHRAM SCHOOL

VPMS Selvi Shankar (SESHA) Vidyashram school was started in June 2018. Unlike other conventional schools and orthodox learning, it explores the child's uniqueness' and introduces them to world class platforms of innovation creativity and leadership in education, sports, arts and wellness.

Mission

Sesha aims at preparing students with skills and knowledge to comprehend, contribute and to succeed in a constantly evolving world. We strive to ensure that our students develop competencies essential for success. Imbibing practical and theoretical knowledge for better understanding of the world. Our students would as an individual, be future contributors towards the local and global communities.

Vision

  • To kindle the curiosity in young minds with a thirst for learning
  • Encourage the children to think independently and become passionate learners
  • To groom children to become capable, responsible, caring and performing citizens in tomorrow's society
