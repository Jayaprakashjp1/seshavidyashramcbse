General Rules

I. DISCIPLINARY RULES

  1. Students are expected to behave in a gentle and courteous manner towards their fellow students and teachers.
  2. Students are responsible for the safe custody of their books and belongings. They are advised not to bring money unnecessarily.
  3. Students are forbidden, to write or scribble on the walls, desks. Any damage done to the school property must be paid for at the discretion of the disciplinary committee.
  4. Unwanted arguments with any staff member in the campus will be seriously dealt with.
  5. Students using foul language, uncouth behavior will be expelled from the school. The disciplinary committee's decision will be final in this regard.
  6. Students are strictly forbidden to ride bikes to the school.
  7. Students using the school van are expected to behave properly. Van services will not be extended to students who misbehave or cause any damage to the van.
  8. Students who go home alone, should not loiter on the way but be prompt in returning home. Parents are asked to have a watch over on this.
